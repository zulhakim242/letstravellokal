letsTravelLokal 2021

Travelling

Phase 1

  • Movement limited to 10km travel radius

  • No travelling between distracts / states / country


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Crossing districts / states allowed; only for married couples and parents to children below 18 years old, with permission from PDRM

  • Citizens and non-citizens who have a residence in Malaysia and arrived from abroad, may have their compulsory quarantine at home

Phase 2

  • Movement limited to 10km travel radius

  • No travelling between distracts / states / country


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Inter-district travels are allowed

  • Crossing states allowed for married couples and parents to children below 18 years old, with permission from PDRM

  • Citizens and non-citizens who have a residence in Malaysia and arrived from abroad, may have their compulsory quarantine at home

Phase 3

  • No travel radius limit

  • ravelling between districts of the same state are allowed


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Crossing states allowed for married couples and parents to children below 18 years old, with permission from PDRM

  • Citizens and non-citizens who have a residence in Malaysia and arrived from abroad, may have their compulsory quarantine at home

Phase 4

  • Inter-state travel is allowed

  • Travelling across countries subject to risk assessment and agreement between countries


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Citizens and non-citizens who have a residence in Malaysia and arrived from abroad, may have their compulsory quarantine at home

Working

Phase 1

  • Public sector limited to 40% capacity at premises subject to the Chief Secretary to the Government’s directive

  • Private sector limited to 60% capacity, for business sectors allowed to operate

  • Agricultural and commodities sector may operate at full capacity

Phase 2

  • Public sector limited to 60% employee attendance at offices subject to the Chief Secretary to the Government’s directive

  • Private sector limited to 80% employee attendance capacity, for business sectors allowed to operate

  • Agricultural and commodities sector may operate at full capacity

Phase 3

  • Public sector limited to 80% employee attendance at offices subject to the Chief Secretary to the Government’s directive

  • Private sector limited to 80% employee attendance capacity, for business sectors allowed to operate

  • Agricultural and commodities sector may operate at full capacity

Phase 4

  • Public sector is allowed to operate 100% subject to the Chief Secretary to the Government’s directive

  • Private sector is allowed to operate 100%

Schooling

Phase 1

  • All educational institutions are closed

  • All learning activities are to be conducted virtually

Phase 2

  • All educational institutions are closed

  • All learning activities are to be conducted virtually

  • Only exam classes can return to school

Phase 3

  • All educational institutions are reopened; institutions under the Ministry of Education will open by stages, subject to risk assessments and the Ministry of Education

  • Face-to-face learning activities are allowed; face-to-face learning activities by higher education institutions are subject to 70% student capacity limits and SOP

Phase 4

  • All educational institutions are reopened, subject to current risk assessment

Eating Out

Phase 1

  • Only takeaways and deliveries are allowed


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Allowed to dine-in at restaurants at 50% table capacity, with physical distancing and SOP compliance

Phase 2

  • Only takeaways and deliveries are allowed


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Allowed to dine-in at restaurants at 50% table capacity, with physical distancing and SOP compliance

Phase 3

  • Dine-in allowed with physical distancing of at least 1 meter

Phase 4

  • Dine-in allowed subject to physical distancing

Phase 1

  • Daily, permanent farmers, controlled fresh, and wholesale markets are open

  • Morning, night, weekly and farmers markets may operate for customers that have been fully vaccinated

Phase 2

  • Temporary farmers market, and morning markets are open

Phase 3

  • All markets are open with SOP

Phase 4

  • All markets are open with SOP

Shopping

Phase 1

  • Only 2 family members in a car are allowed to buy necessities within a 10km radius

Phase 2

  • Only 2 family members in a car are allowed to buy necessities within a 10km radius

Phase 3

  • Only 3 family members in a car are allowed to buy necessities

Phase 4

  • Number of people in a car is determined by the car's capacity

Sports

Phase 1

  • Jogging, cycling and exercising individually are allowed with physical distancing and within the neighbourhood area


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Allowed to perform non-contact sports in open / semi-open areas without radius limits and with physical distancing; 6am-10pm

Phase 2

  • Outdoor non-contact sports are allowed within a 10km radius from home subject to SOP


    • Fully Vaccinated Individuals
  • Allowed to perform non-contact sports in open / semi-open areas without radius limits and with physical distancing; 6am-10pm

Phase 3

  • All non-contact sports are allowed subject to SOP

Phase 4

  • All sports are allowed subject to SOP
