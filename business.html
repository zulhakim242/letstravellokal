Opium KL

Voted as one of the most alluring restaurant and bar in Bukit Bintang, Opium KL serves up delicious Asian cuisine with a twist. The folks at Opium inject creativity in every item on their menu, with dishes like Curry Spaghetti, Beef Kut Teh Noodles, Deep-Fried Loh Mai Kai, and Angus Beef Rendang. They're also popular for their all-day dim sums (with dim sum buffets on some days!) and handcrafted cocktails. For those heading over for a romantic dinner date, we'd recommend reserving one of their couple-style dens protected by red veils for an intimate meal with your loved ones.
